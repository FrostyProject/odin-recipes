Dad's Jumpin Jumbalia

Description

Hands-down the best jambalaya recipe! It is surprisingly easy to make, customizable with your favorite proteins (I used chicken, shrimp and Andouille sausage), and full of bold, zesty, Cajun jambalaya flavors that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • Cajun seasoning
  • Jalapeno
  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Chicken stock
  • White rice
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Saute the chicken
  2. Saute the veggies
  3. Add rice and chicken stock
  4. Cover and cook for 30 minutes
  5. Taste and season
  6. Enjoy
